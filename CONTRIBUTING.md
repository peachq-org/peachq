# Contributing to PeachQ

Thank you for your interest in PeachQ. Seeing people want to contribute is one of the best parts of building an open-source project.

## We'd love your help

PeachQ is still in its early days and there are lots of ways to contribute.

In particular, we'd love to hear from:

- **IDE and editor authors** — let's make existing q tooling work beautifully with PeachQ.
- **Library authors** — interested in helping build an open standard library or reusable packages?
- **People with real-world q code** — compatibility test cases are incredibly valuable.
- **Documentation writers** — tutorials, examples and migration guides.
- **Tool builders** — language servers, formatters, linters, drivers, notebooks and integrations.
- **Companies using q** — tell us what matters most to you.
- **Anyone excited by the idea of an open q ecosystem.**

If any of that sounds interesting, **please get in touch first**. We'd love to chat about how you can get involved.
